Disha Yoga Classes

What types of yoga classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of classes, including Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa Flow, and Mindful Meditation



How do I register for a class?

Visit our Classes page to view the schedule and simply click on the class you'd like to attend. Follow the registration instructions provided.



Are your classes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our classes cater to practitioners of all levels, including beginners. Our instructors provide modifications to suit individual needs.



Are private sessions available?

Yes, we offer private sessions with our experienced instructors. Contact us to discuss your goals and schedule a personalized session.



Can pregnant individuals participate in your classes?

Yes, many of our classes are suitable for pregnant individuals. However, it's important to consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new exercise routine. Our instructors can also provide modifications as needed.



Are online classes available?

Yes, we offer online classes for those who prefer to practice from the comfort of their home.



What is the age range for your classes?

We welcome practitioners of all ages. While some classes may have age recommendations, we offer a variety of sessions suitable for children, teenagers, adults, and seniors.
